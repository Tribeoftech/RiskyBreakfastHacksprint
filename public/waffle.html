Waffle

Pros Cons
Fluffy waffles: Pillow-like clouds of deliciousness that melt in your mouth. Waffle stickiness: Sometimes the syrup-to-waffle ratio results in sticky fingers and a syrupy mess.
Waffle art: A canvas for creating edible masterpieces with toppings like fruits, chocolate, and sprinkles. Waffle debates: The eternal question of whether square or round waffles are superior can spark passionate discussions
